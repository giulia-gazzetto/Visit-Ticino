Why Ticino

Ticino, in all his beauty

Legend has it that Ticino, nestled between majestic mountains and serene lakes, has long been a meeting point of cultures, where Swiss precision meets Mediterranean warmth. Once a land of ancient settlements and Roman roads, it flourished as a bridge between northern and southern Europe.

Today, traces of Ticinoâ€™s storied past blend seamlessly with its modern charm. Beyond the elegant piazzas lined with pastel-hued buildings, the bustling markets selling local cheeses and wines, and the lakeside promenades where life moves at a leisurely pace, you can still wander through medieval villages, explore centuries-old castles, and take in breathtaking views of the Alps reflected in crystal-clear waters.

Where to go

The best spots in Ticino

Lugano and Lake Lugano

A charming lakeside city with scenic parks, elegant architecture, and breathtaking mountain views. Perfect for a relaxing stroll along the waterfront or a fun day out with activities ranging from boat rides to hiking trails.

Learn more

Toce Waterfalls

A stunning natural wonder in a pristine valley, where crystal-clear waters cascade through lush landscapes. A must-see for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts, perfect for hiking and enjoying the fresh mountain air.

Learn more

Bellinzona and its Castles

A historic town famous for its medieval castles, which are UNESCO World Heritage sites, set against a backdrop of dramatic mountains. A perfect destination for history buffs and those who appreciate timeless architecture.
